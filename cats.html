All about my Cats

We never thought we'd have cats, let alone 3 of them. It all started when we went to find a cat for my Nana.
When we went to collect the cat she chose online we couldn't help but adopt 2 others: Whiskers and Tigger. Since they've been part of the family.
A couple years later we adopted Ollie after thinking she was a stray in fact one of the neighbours just didn't feed her, so now she's ours.

The Cats

Tigger

Her name comes from the character 'Tigger' from Winnie the Pooh because she bounces around when she's happy

Likes
- Sleeping on my bed - lint rollers at the ready!
- Bouncing around the garden
- Bringing in massive moths to play with
Head and Ear scratches
Dislikes
- The Hoover
- Fireworks
- Ollie - But I think she secretly likes her

Whiskers

He gets his name from looking like the Whiskers cat in the adverts

Likes

- Head pats, ear scratches, belly rubs, Pet all the places!

Dislikes
- Loud noises - literally scared of his own shadow
- When you ignore him, he'll meow. Loudly
- Other cats being in his garden, he'll chase them off if he sees them

Ollie

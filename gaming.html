All my Gaming Adventures!

This page will show you all of my current and favourite gaming adventures from World of Warcraft to Genshin Impact!

World of Warcraft

I've played this game for 14 years, geez what a long, strange trip It's been
I've always played multiple roles with Tanking being my main, starting with a Rouge called DaRealDeal and boy was I! Then onto Blood Death Knight called Drjinxablok when the Lich King Expansion came out, 14 year old me was proud of that name. I ended up with the recent expansion maining a Protection Paladin and playing Restoration Druid and Fire/Frost Mage on the side whenever my back breaks from carrying people so much Personally, I'm not much of a Coop player these days, back in the day I was what you'd called a "Hardcore Raider" and Lead a fairly respectable guild. Nowadays, I stick to Solo Content, Achievement, Mount and Pet hunting and general exploration are my favourites. Along with the odd money making here and there
As of June 2021 however, my journey in Shadowlands ended but who knows I may return sooner than I think.
If you stare long enough into the Abyss, the Abyss stares back...

Runescape

I've played this game for 17 years, from the age of 11 and it was my first MMO experience. The casual and calming way Runescape is played is always fun to me, even now while typing this I'm doing some Archaeology on it!
Some would say Runescape these days is outdated and past it's prime but I love nothing more than to sink my self into the world and complete some questions, slay some monsters and chill with achievement hunting. Although I don't play Runescape nearly as much as I used to I'm active on mainy Discord servers helping and chatting with anyone that comes my way.

Genshin Impact

This game is marvelous, i've played it since day one and always love exploring the new content they add, playing the latest event and failing at getting character I want
I main Ameno Traveler, Qiqi, Fischl and switch between Diluc, Hu Tao and Klee for my dps with Xingqui, Xiao and Noelle as added support as an added support
I mainly play solo in this game, not because I'm antisocial but mostly I like getting lost in the open world and sitting down and reading all the dialogue and expericing the story as much as possible.
Except for the Spiral Abyss, we could delete that and I wouldn't care at all
